Frequently Asked Questions

How long does paint protection film last? +

High-quality PPF lasts 5 to 10 years, protecting your vehicle from everyday wear, environmental damage, and aging.

Will the film affect my car's original paint color? +

No, PPF is optically clear and preserves your factory paint while enhancing gloss and shine.

Can the film be removed later? +

Yes, PPF can be safely removed by professionals without damaging the original paint.

Does PPF require special maintenance? +

No special care needed. Just regular washing. Many premium films even self-heal light scratches with heat.
